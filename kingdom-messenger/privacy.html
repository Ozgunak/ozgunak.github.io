Privacy Policy — Kingdom Messenger

Effective May 1, 2026

Kingdom Messenger (the "App") is published by Ozgun Aksoy ("we", "us"). This page describes what information the App handles, what it does not, and how to contact us with questions.

The short version

  • The App does not require an account, an email address, or any sign-up.
  • The App does not contain advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or any third-party trackers.
  • Your save game and high-score history live only on your device.
  • The only times data leaves your device are: in-app purchases (handled by Apple), Game Center scores and achievements (handled by Apple), and bug reports or surveys you choose to submit through optional in-app forms.

What the App handles

In-app purchases. Kingdom Messenger offers optional non-consumable purchases (Full Story, Endless Mode, Royal Patron) processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment details. Apple confirms whether you own a purchase so we can unlock the corresponding feature.

Game Center. If you sign in to Apple Game Center, the App can submit your career and Endless leaderboard scores and report achievements you have unlocked. This information is handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. The App itself does not store your Game Center identifier or your real name.

Bug reports and player surveys. The App contains two optional forms: a bug-report form and a player survey. If you choose to submit either, the text you type is sent to a Google Form managed by us. Submissions may include your written description, the day you reached, difficulty rating, and similar gameplay context. We use this information solely to improve the game. Submissions are processed under Google's Privacy Policy. Do not include personal information in these forms; only submit what you are comfortable sharing.

Save data and settings. Your save file, local high-score list, and preferences are written to your device's local storage and never uploaded to us. They are removed when you delete the App.

What the App does NOT do

  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics SDKs and no behavioral tracking.
  • No collection of your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or location.
  • No use of Apple's IDFA or App Tracking Transparency tracking.
  • No selling or sharing of any data with marketers or data brokers.

Children

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App contains no chat, no online matchmaking, and no user-to-user communication. Bug reports and surveys are optional, and a parent or guardian should supervise their child if they choose to submit one. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

Data security

Information transmitted from the App to Apple (purchases, Game Center) and to Google (forms) is sent over HTTPS. Local save data is stored in your device's app sandbox.

Your rights

Because the App does not collect personal information, there is normally nothing to access or delete. If you have submitted a bug report or survey and want it removed, contact us with enough detail to identify the submission and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy in a meaningful way, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or concerns: kingdommessengergame@gmail.com
